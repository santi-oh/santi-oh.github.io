Well That Was Good
(2018–ongoing)
Collaborative project Well That Was Good was conceived as a response to foodporn.

Instead of portraying beautifully staged meals, it focuses on the experience itself by displaying the aftermath—empty dishes in their original settings—thus inviting the viewer to fill in the gaps and eventually give into their curiosity & visit the restaurants.
