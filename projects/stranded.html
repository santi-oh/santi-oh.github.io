Stranded is a photo series about clothing abandoned in public spaces. The images hint at speculative urban tales and prompt the viewer to question the origin of these garments and the fate of their owners.

The series tries to reconstruct narratives based on how the objects interact with their surroundings. Each image prompts the viewer to question who the previous owner could have been and the circumstances in which they were separated from their belongings. All these personal item are portrayed exactly as they were found.
